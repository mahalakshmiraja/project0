Millennium Park

Millennium Park- A spectacular gathering spot in the heart of the city. The park area is 24.5 acres. It has many attractions to look around. You can easily spend two dys exploring the city. There are many live concerts, water fountain for kids to play, evening fireworks at Navy Pier.

Main Attractions in Millennium Park:

  • Cloud Gate
  • The Art Institute of Chicago
  • Navy Pier
  • Buckingham Fountain
  • Jay Pritzker Pavilion
  • The Field Museum
  • Adler Planetarium

The Field Museum and Adler Planetarium are south on the lake front
